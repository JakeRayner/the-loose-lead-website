Our Services

Every dog is different, that's why we offer a range of services to suit your pet's needs and your schedule.

🐾  Group Walk

A fantastic way for your dog to socialise, burn off energy, and enjoy a proper outdoor adventure - all in the company of carefully matched doggy companions.

Every dog in the group is individually cared for, so your pup always feels seen, safe, and well looked after.

✓ Post-walk Report

🐕  Solo Walk

Your dog gets our undivided attention – perfect for nervous pups, dogs in training, or simply those who prefer to go at their own pace.

Solo walks are tailored entirely around your dog's needs, energy level, and favourite routes.

✓ 100% undivided focus ✓ Flexible routes ✓ Great for anxious dogs

💍  Wedding Chaperone

Want your four-legged best friend to be part of your special day? We've got them covered.

We'll take care of your dog from start to finish, getting them looking their best, bringing them to the ceremony at just the right moment, keeping them calm and happy throughout, and looking after them once the celebrations begin.

✓ Collection & drop-off ✓ On-site throughout ✓ Ceremony attendance ✓ Stress-Free ✓ Tailored to your schedule

🏠  Pet Holiday Visit

We'll come to your home and check your pets are safe, happy, and comfortable in their own familiar surroundings – no upheaval, no stress.

Perfect for dogs, cats, and small animals alike.

✓ Dogs, cats & small animals ✓ Daily visit ✓ Feed & fresh water ✓ Photo & video updates

🐶  Puppy Visit

A new puppy is an exciting time, but they need plenty of company and care throughout the day.

We pop in to check on your pup, keep them stimulated, and make sure they're not left alone for too long while they're still settling in.

✓ Daytime check-ins ✓ Playtime & socialisation ✓ Toilet breaks ✓ Feeding if needed ✓ Photo updates

🧡  Personalised Care Packages

Every dog and family is different, so we offer bespoke care packages built around your exact schedule, your pet's needs, and your lifestyle.

Whether you need a mix of walks, visits, and weekend cover, or something completely unique, we'll put together a package that works perfectly for you.

About Us

We're The Loose Lead Co.

We're dog people, plain and simple. We started The Loose Lead Co. because we believe every dog deserves walks that are relaxed, stimulating and full of joy — not stressful or rushed.

We're based in Staffordshire and cover Staffordshire and Cheshire, taking pride in getting to know each dog individually. Whether your pup is a social butterfly or a solo explorer, a bouncy puppy or a gentle senior, we tailor every visit to what makes them happiest.

We're fully insured, passionate about animal welfare, and committed to building genuine, long-lasting relationships with both our four-legged clients and their families.

Professional, loving pet care where your dog sets the pace. Based locally, trusted by dog families across the area.
